Sound

Pixabay

As someone who is not an audio engineer, it can be hard figuring out how to make your own sounds for your games. Usually, it may be a better to get stock sound effects off of the Internet. A site you may find such sound effects on is Pixabay. I have found this site useful for aquiring various sound effects for my games. You can find all sorts of sound assets here from hits, beeps, bops, sounds, and music.

Zapsplat

Zapsplat is another site you can use to find free sound effects. If Pixabay does not meet your needs then you may want to try this one. On Zapsplat you can find royalty free music, effects packs, and various other assets. Overall, this is a very useful resource for meeting your audio needs!

Retro Sound Effect Generator

This website is a web port of the sfxr sound effect generator by "DrPetter". You can track down the original software or use this website. On the website you can generate all sorts of retro game sound effects. There are all sorts of settings and toggles you can mess around with until you find something satisfactory. I have used this website for a very long time, it has been quite helpful.
