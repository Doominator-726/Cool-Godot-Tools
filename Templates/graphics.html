Convert Videos Into the .ogv Format

I like using videos in my Godot games. Unfortunately, right now, Godot only supports the ogv (ogg video) format. I have had a lot of trouble getting mp4 videos to play in Godot. I believe that there are some extensions that can help you play the regular file formats, but I have found two websites that can help with getting ogg videos.
First is convertio.co, which I have found to do a good job at converting my mp4s to ogvs. The notable thing about it is that it did not ruin my videos during conversion where other websites have. The downside is that there is a file limit if you do not have the paid version.
Aside from that you can use freeconvert.com or the a VLC media player, but these ruined my videos with compression when I tried them.

Make Space Skyboxes

Using the tool, Space 3D from wwwtyro.net you can make various space skyboxes for your game! In the program, you can alter the FOV and the presence of various objects like the sun and stars. Notably, the website exports cubemaps, so to turn them into panoramas, you will need to see the next tool.

Turn Cubemaps into Panoramas

This is a tool, that allows you to convert cubemaps, such as the one seen to the left, into proper panoramas. You just upload your files into the website, and the website returns a panorama. If you are using this in conjunction with the last tool, then you may find figuring out what files to upload where confusing. Luckily, I have mapped it out. The skybox generator's "up" and "down" files correspond to the "top" and "bottom" slots respectively. Furthermore, the "back" and "front" files correspond to the "left" and "right" slots.
